RawGraphs Lab

RawGraphs is a free and open-source online tool to visualize data. Through RawGraphs you can input data and create a over 25 different types of charts. I think RawGraphs is useful for exploring new graphs and to quickly visualize data in multiple formats to find the best fit. However, it is difficult to customize the graphs so for a final graph I may try to utilize a program that is better for making artistic adjustments.
